This will be the third in the series of meetings organized by the Wales Institute of Mathematical and Computational Sciences (WIMCS) during 2014, sponsored by WIMCS and the London Mathematical Society. The meeting, which is open to all, will provide an opportunity to early career researchers in analysis, numerical methods and applications to present their recent results.

Two previous meetings took place in Cardiff and Aberystwyth. This third meeting will take place in Swansea starting at 10:00 on Thursday 25th Septmebr 2014 and the Scientific Programme will end by 17:30 on the same day. There will be a dinner on Thursday evening.
