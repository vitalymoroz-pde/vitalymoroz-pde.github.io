\title{ On fractional Sobolev, Isoperimetric and Poincar\'{e} inequalities}
\author{Gyula Csat\'{o} (Barcelona)}
\date{}


\maketitle

\thispagestyle{empty}

\noindent
{\bf Abstract.} My aim is to give a comprehensive overview on fractional Sobolev, Isoperimetric and Poincaré Inequalities. I will explain some analogies and differences with the corresponding local inequalities, symmetrization techniques, best constant problems, and other current challenges. If time permits, I shall briefly mention in the end one recent result on regional fractional Poincaré inequality on unbounded domains (work in collaboration with Chowdhury I., Firoz S. and Roy P.).
