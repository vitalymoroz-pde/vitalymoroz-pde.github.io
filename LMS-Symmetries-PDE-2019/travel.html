Local Information

The event will take place in Mathematics Department of Swansea University, which is located in Computational Foundry, our new home on the Bay Campus of Swansea University, No.10 on the Bay Campus Map.

  • Download Bay Campus Map
All invited speakers will be staying in Premier Inn Swansea Waterfront hotel. A bus stop for Unibus is just a short walk from the hotel. Buses run every 10min or so and the journey to Bay Campus takes about 10min.

Travelling from Airports

From London Heathrow, the quickest option is to take RailAir coach service from Heathrow to Reading railway station, from where there are hourly direct train services to Swansea. Alternatively, take the Heathrow Express train to London Paddington station, from where there are regular direct trains to Swansea.

From London Gatwick and London Heathrow there is a direct National Express coach to Swansea.  Alternatively, From London Gatwick take a train to Reading for connections to Swansea.

From London Stansted there are train services to London Liverpool St. This is connected, via the underground network, to London Paddington from which there are frequent services to Swansea. 

From Cardiff Airport, take a bus or taxi to Cardiff City Centre for train links to Swansea. Alternative detailed instructions on travelling from Cardiff Airport to Swansea can be found here. 

Bristol Airport is linked by a regular coach service to Bristol Temple Meads railway station, from where there are hourly train services to Swansea (change at Bristol Parkway or Cardiff Central).

For train times and bookings, consult www.nationalrail.co.uk or www.thetrainline.com

Local transportation

If arriving by train or coach, the easiest option is to take a taxi either to your hotel or to the Bay Campus of Swansea University. It takes about 20 minutes to reach the Bay Campus.

Travelling by Car

Bay Campus postcode for satnav is SA1 8EN. Visitors car park is labelled P1 on the Campus Map.
