Recommended books

A book by J. C. Robinson, An Introduction to Ordinary Differential Equations (Cambridge University Press, 2004) is could be recommended as the principal reading for the theoretical part of the course. Our Lecture Notes and Problem Sheets follow very closely first chapters of this book.

A book by M. L. Abell and J. P. Braselton, Differential Equations with MATHEMATICA (Academic Press, any edition) would make a good reference for the computational part of the course.

A book by B. F. Torrence and E. A. Torrence, The Student's Introduction to MATHEMATICA (Cambridge University Press, any edition) is a good elementary introduction into MATHEMATICA.
