Registration

There is no registration fee associated with attending the School; however those planning to attend are asked please to register.

Please, register by Monday 7th of December 2009 by sending an email to Yelena Liskevich, containing the following information:

  1. Name
  2. Email address
  3. Institution
  4. If you need a parking permit on Campus
  5. If you plan to attend the dinner on Friday evening (cost £30)
  6. If you have any special requirements (e.g. dietary requirements, wheelchair access)
  7. If you are a postgraduate student, please state additionally your supervisor and:
    • whether you would like to apply for financial support
    • whether you would like to make a short (20 min) contributed presentation
    If you want to make a short presentation then submit the title and abstartct of your proposed talk.
Presentations will be selected by the Local Organizers and applicants notified in early December.

Financial support

Limited funds are available to contribute to local expenses of UK research students and junior researchers.

The deadline to apply for financial support is Monday 7th of December. Decisions will be made and applicants notified soon thereafter.
