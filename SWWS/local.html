Local Information

The event will take place on campus of Swansea University. As Wales's second city, Swansea has a full range of social, cultural and sporting amenities and excellent transport links. Founded in 1920, Swansea University enjoys exceptional location in parkland overlooking Swansea Bay on the edge of the Gower Peninsula, Britain's first 'Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty'.
