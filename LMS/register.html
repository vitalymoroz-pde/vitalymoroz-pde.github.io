Registration

Registration

The course is primarily aimed at postgraduate students in PDEs or related fields but much of it should be accessible to anyone with a reasonable knowledge of basic concepts in Analysis. Postdocs and young researchers are welcome to attend.

  • All research students registered at a UK university will be charged a registration fee of £100 which includes the full subsistence cost of accommodation and meals. (In the case of EPSRC funded research students, this fee should be paid by their departments from their DTA)

  • Students from the EU coutries participating in The Global Network will be charged £425 (registration fee of £100 plus full subsistence costs of £325 which includes accomodation and meals).

  • All other overseas students, postdocs and those working in industry must pay a registration fee of £250, plus the full subsistence costs of £325, making a total of £575 for this course.

Funding
  • For students from the EU coutries participating in The Global Network there will be a limited number of grants provided by the ESF via The Global Network. The grants will cover registration and subsistence cost. If you are interested in being considered please, tick the relevant box on the application form.

  • For other non-UK junior particpants there will be a small number of grants provided by the Wales Institute of Mathematical and Computer Sciences. The grants will cover registration and subsistence cost. If you are interested in being considered please, tick the relevant box on the application form.

  • All participants must pay their own travel costs (for EPSRC funded students, this should be covered by their DTA).


How to apply

Applications should be made using the registration form available on the London Mathematical Society website at: www.lms.ac.uk/activities/rmc/sc/39poster.html

Numbers will be limited and those interested are advised to make an early application. The closing date for applications is Friday 9 May. All applicants will be contacted by the London Mathematical Society approximately one week after the deadline; we will not be able to give information about individual applications before then.
