Registration

There is no registration fee associated with attending either the LMS meeting or the workshop.

Those planning to attend only the LMS meeting on 15th September need only register if you would like to attend the dinner on Monday 15th or to let us know of any special requirements.

Those planning to attend the workshop are asked please to register.

Please, register by 8th September by sending an email to Elaine Crooks, containing the following information:

  1. Name
  2. Email address
  3. Institution
  4. If you are a postgraduate student, please state your supervisor and whether you would like to apply for financial support
  5. If you plan to attend the dinner on Monday evening (cost £30)
  6. If you have any special requirements (e.g. dietary requirements, wheelchair access)

The deadline for postgraduate students to apply for financial support is Monday 25th August. Decisions will be made and applicants notified soon thereafter.

Accommodation bookings should be made by attendees (invited speakers will be contacted separately about their accommodation arrangements). Some rooms on campus are available and can be booked directly via this website. Alternatively, a list of local guest houses and hotels is available at the website here.
