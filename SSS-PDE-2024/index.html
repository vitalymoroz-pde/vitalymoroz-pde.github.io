Swansea Summer School in Nonlinear PDEs

1 – 5 July 2024, Swansea University

This Summer School, part of the EPSRC network in Generalised and low-regularity solutions for nonlinear PDEs, aims to enhance the training of early career researchers in the field through mini-courses and guest lectures on topics ranging across nonlinear PDEs, and to offer early career researchers the opportunity to interact with leaders in their fields, expose them to adjacent fields, and build networks and collaborations among themselves.

Minicourse lecturers

  • Martino Bardi (Università degli Studi di Padova, Italy)
  • Fully nonlinear degenerate elliptic equations: qualitative properties of viscosity solutions
  • Serena Dipierro (University of Western Australia)
  • Nonlocal equations and applications
  • Louis Jeanjean (Université de Franche-Comté, France)
  • On nonlinear quantum graphs
  • Enrico Valdinoci (University of Western Australia)
  • Nonlocal minimal surfaces and phase transitions
  • Jean Van Schaftingen (UCLouvain, Belgium)
  • Sobolev mappings into manifolds

Guest lecturers

  • John Ball (Heriot-Watt University)
  • Juan Davila (University of Bath)
  • Nicolas Dirr (Cardiff University)
  • Xue-Mei Li (Imperial College London)
  • Florian Theil (University of Warwick)

Registration for the School will take place in the ground floor foyer of the Computational Foundry and be open from 8.30am on Monday.

Scam warning

We are aware of scams targeting international research event participants. Scammers may contact you posing as third-party booking agents. Do not provide any personal or payment information. All bookings are handled directly by Swansea University or Cardiff University. If you are unsure about an email or call, please contact one of the organizers for verification.
