Swansea Summer School in Nonlinear PDEs

Venue

All lectures will be held in Room 002, Department of Mathematics, Computational Foundry Building (No 11 on Bay Campus map), Swansea University Bay Campus, Swansea SA1 8EN.
Registration for the School will take place in the ground floor foyer of the Computational Foundry and be open from 8.30am on Monday.
