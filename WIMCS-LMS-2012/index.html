The WIMCS - LMS Workshop on Calculus of Variations and Nonlinear PDEs will be held at Swansea University on Friday 25th of May 2012. The Workshop will address new trends in the modern theory and applications of the Calculus of Variations and Nonlinear PDEs.

There will be a dinner on the Friday evening.

Invited Lectures
  • Norman Dancer (Sydney, Australia)
    Large interaction problems for nonlinear elliptic and parabolic partial differential equations

  • Augusto Ponce (Louvaine-la-Neuve, Belgium)
    The divergence of continuous vector fields

  • Konstantinos Koumatos (Oxford, UK)
    Quasiconvexity at the boundary and the nucleation of austenite

  • Jeyabal Sivaloganathan (Bath, UK)
    Symmetry of energy minimising equilibria in nonlinear elasticity


The Workshop is a part of the series of the WIMCS - LMS Analysis Workshops, supported by the Wales Institute of Mathematical and Computational Sciences, the London Mathematical Society and Oxford Centre for Nonlinear PDE
