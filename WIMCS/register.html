Registration

There is no registration fee associated with attending the Workshop; however those planning to attend are asked please to register.

Please, register by sending an email to Yelena Liskevich, containing the following information:

  1. Name
  2. Email address
  3. Institution
  4. If you need a parking permit on Campus
  5. If you plan to attend the dinner on Friday evening (cost £25)
  6. If you have any special requirements (e.g. dietary requirements, wheelchair access)
If you are a postgraduate student, please state additionally your supervisor and whether you would like to apply for financial support.

Financial support

Limited funds are available to contribute to travel expenses of UK research students.

The deadline to apply for financial support is the 9th of May. Decisions will be made and applicants notified soon thereafter.
