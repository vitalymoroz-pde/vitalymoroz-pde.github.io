The WIMCS - LMS Workshop on Calculus of Variations and Nonlinear PDEs will be held at Swansea University on Friday 20th of May 2011. The Workshop will address new trends in the modern theory and applications of the Calculus of Variations and Nonlinear PDEs.

There will be a dinner on the Friday evening.

Invited Lectures
  • John Toland (Bath, UK)
    Waves with prescribed distribution of vorticity

  • Veronica Felli (Milan, Italy)
    Monotonicity methods for asymptotics of solutions to elliptic and parabolic equations near singularities of the potential

  • Jean Dolbeault (CEREMADE Paris, France)
    Existence, symmetry and symmetry breaking for extremal functions of some interpolation functional inequalities

  • Gui-Qiang Chen (Oxford, UK)
    Nonlinear Partial Differential Equations of Mixed Elliptic-Hyperbolic Type

Slides of all the talks could be downloaded under the links above.


The Workshop is a part of the series of the WIMCS - LMS Analysis Workshops, supported by the Wales Institute of Mathematical and Computational Sciences and the London Mathematical Society.
