\noindent
{\bf Abstract.}
In this talk, we summarize the known facts for the bounds of
the Sobolev norm for solutions of semilinear parabolic problems with
critical Sobolev exponent and
try to apply the idea of profile-decomposition to obtain some
information about the asymptotics of the Sobolev norm of solutions.
