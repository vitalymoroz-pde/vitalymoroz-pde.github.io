Registration

All external participants except the invited speakers need to register.

Registration fees of £50 for the entire event, or £20 per day, will be charged to external participants except for the invited speakers. The registration fees will be used to cover additional catering expenses (lunches and refreshments).

Financial support

Limited funds are available from the LMS to contribute to the support of UK based PhD students.

Support is also available for childcare costs of the participants.

Register/apply for financial support here before 1st May.

Decisions about the financial support will be made soon thereafter.

Poster session

If you would like to participate in the poster session please submit the title and abstract of your poster by 1st May by email to v.moroz@swansea.ac.uk or e.c.m.crooks@swansea.ac.uk
